Our Missions

Scaling up business

  • To develop business’ owned by stakeholders in a blooming market with unlimited network
  • To provide stakeholders with matching collaborations based on their interests and needs
  • To create a free advertisement platform for stakeholders
  • To improve business’ credibility and reputation

Expressing new ideas through events!

  • To incentivize users in expressing and sharing fresh ideas that can be brought to life through events
  • To encourage users in thinking critically and globally in creating top-quality events that are beneficial for the community

Nurturing as family in social community

  • To create a meeting point of users with the same interest within a community where everyone can share their idea freely
  • To create a social community that acts as a family for every members in it
  • To provide an aspiration forum for people who want to create a better networking between members of social community

Creating unlimited jobs for everyone!

  • To match eventmakers with freelancers based on their needs and skills
  • To promote freelancing as a new job trend
  • To provide more jobs in Indonesia
  • To provide credibility of freelancers
